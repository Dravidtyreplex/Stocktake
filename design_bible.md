# 📘 91Wheels Brand Design Bible
## Visual Identity & Design Guidelines (Version 1.0)

This is the official **91Wheels Design Bible** based on the 2025 Brand Guidelines. It defines the brand's core values, visual identity, color tokens, typography scales, visual patterns, iconography, and media guidelines to ensure a consistent, premium user experience across all web, mobile, and social platforms.

---

## 👁️ Brand Vision & Essence

### 1. Our Vision
> **Simplify Personal Mobility**  
> We make personal mobility simple, accessible, and transparent through **Trust**, **Personalisation**, and **Innovation**.

### 2. Our Purpose (Why We Exist)
To bridge the gap between automotive complexity and user clarity, empowering India’s mobile-first generation with intelligent, personalized guidance that transforms complex vehicle decisions into confident, trustworthy, value-driven choices.

### 3. Core Tagline
> **Smart Choice. Best Value.**  
> Represents the ultimate balance of making an intelligent vehicle decision while securing the maximum value for the user's budget.

## 🎨 Color Palette & Swatches

Our brand colors project reliability, energy, and professionalism. Color codes must be applied exactly as specified, without alteration or opacity blending (unless explicitly defined in shades).

### 1. Primary Colors
*   **Nitro Blue (`#0165FE`):** The primary energy-driving brand color representing connectivity and tech-forward innovation.
    *   *RGB:* `1, 101, 255` | *CMYK:* `81, 61, 0, 0`
*   **Midnight Dark (`#142338`):** The core background, text, and structure color representing security, authority, and depth.
    *   *RGB:* `20, 35, 56` | *CMYK:* `92, 79, 50, 58`

### 2. Secondary Accent Colors
*   **Sporty Yellow (`#F9D816`):** Highlights premium rewards, metrics, warning highlights, or active tags.
    *   *RGB:* `249, 216, 22` | *CMYK:* `4, 11, 98, 0`
*   **Steel Grey (`#A9AFB5`):** Standard neutral card outlines, borders, and disabled indicators.
    *   *RGB:* `169, 175, 181` | *CMYK:* `35, 25, 23, 0`

### 3. Neutral Shades & Backgrounds
*   **Titanium Grey (`#43525E`):** Secondary header text, card secondary details.
*   **Asphalt Grey (`#798189`):** Body copy text, secondary icons.
*   **Alloy Silver (`#A9AFB5`):** Divider lines and borders.
*   **Frost Silver (`#DAE1E5`):** Light page backgrounds, card backdrops.

## ✍️ Typography & Hierarchical Scale

Our typographic scale establishes structure and legibility across layouts. 

### 1. Primary Font: Poppins
**Poppins** is our primary font, reflecting our modern, dynamic, and digital-first identity. It is used across all web, mobile, and print assets.
*   **Headlines & Hero Text:** `Poppins Bold`
*   **Subheadings & Form Labels:** `Poppins SemiBold`
*   **Body & Explanatory Text:** `Poppins Regular`

### 2. Secondary Font: Helvetica
**Helvetica** is our secondary/system font. It is reserved for email communications, notifications, or instances where custom web fonts (Poppins) cannot load.

### 3. Visual Type Scale
*   **Headlines (24px - 36px Bold):** High-emphasis titles and hero section headers.
*   **Subheads (16px - 20px SemiBold):** Intermediate section titles and card headings.
*   **Body Copy (14px Regular):** Core paragraphs, list items, and descriptions.
*   **Captions/Metadata (10px - 12px Regular/Medium):** Labels, dates, and micro-metrics.

## 📐 Logo Specifications & Usages

The 91Wheels logo mark is a hand-crafted icon merging a circular **"9"** and **"1"** line format.

### 1. Logo Variations
*   **Primary Logo:** Horizontal lockup of Logo Mark + `91Wheels` wordmark.
*   **Primary Logo with Tagline:** Horizontal lockup with tagline `Smart Choice. Best Value.` centered below the wordmark.
*   **Vertical Logo:** Logo Mark stacked above `91Wheels` wordmark.
*   **Vertical Logo with Tagline:** Stacked vertical layout including the tagline.

### 2. Logo Clear Space Guidelines
*   A minimum clear space of **50% of the logo mark width (X)** must be kept clear around all sides of the logo lockups.
*   The vertical height parameter is restricted by the **x-height** of the wordmark letters.

## 🌀 Visual Pattern & Iconography

### 1. The Visual Ribbon
Our signature visual asset is a **curving blue/white ribbon line** representing connectivity, pathing, and smooth personal mobility.
*   *Usage:* Used as organic graphic borders, background framing layers, and thumbnail accent shapes.
*   *Variations:* Available in Nitro Blue on white, White on Nitro Blue, or Light Blue on Midnight Dark.

### 2. Minimal Line Iconography
Our icons use a **clean, minimal line-based style** with balanced proportions and rounded joints (`2px` stroke weight).
*   *Themes:* Automotive elements (cars, bikes, EV plug, tyre tread, CNG tags, speedometers, gear shifters, steering wheels, and wrenches).

## 📸 Photography Guidelines

*   **Vehicle Hero Images:** Cars and bikes must be shot or placed against a neutral studio background composed of a **65% White to 35% Asphalt Grey gradient**.
*   **Angles:** Central placement with consistent three-quarter angles to emphasize headlight design and vehicle depth.
*   **Interiors:** High-precision detail shots (stitching, dashboard screens, center console dial) with clean, crisp lighting.
