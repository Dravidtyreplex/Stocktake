1. Brand Vision & Purpose

Tyreplex is the technology platform driving modern tyre retail and B2B distributions. We believe in providing tyre dealers, store managers, and relationship managers with intuitive, highly visual digital tools that simplify stocktakes, pricing requests, and inventory audits.

Digitize & Empower Tyre Retail
We make tyre operations clean, transparent, and data-driven by replacing paper receipts and manual calculations with modern inventory, B2B bidding, and offline stock audits.

Core Voice Archetypes

Archetype Weight Attributes Meaning for Creative Expression
The Partner 50% Collaborative, Helpful, Localized Relatable, supportive, down-to-earth. Speaks simply and clearly about daily operations.
The Organizer 30% Systematic, Precise, Reliable Clean, structured, detailed. Standardizes specifications (DOT, section widths) into simple layouts.
The Innovator 20% Tech-forward, Proactive, Efficient Pioneering and active. Introduces OCR invoice scanning and live quote countdowns to speed up operations.

2. Color Palette & Functional Swatches

Our colors represent action, success, and alerts. They are optimized for immediate recognition on mobile screens inside high-density warehouses and retail shops.

Primary Colors

Tyreplex Red #ED1D24 Primary logo, primary buttons, shortage counts
Tyreplex Dark #121726 Main header text, active tabs, dark selectors

Functional & Neutral Swatches

Success Green #00B633 Verified stock counts, positive variance, payments
Bidding Orange #FF6B00 Live timers, alerts, warning counts, quantities
Info Blue #0066FF SKU categories, BNPL information panels
Frost Canvas Backdrop #F7FAFA Backdrop behind cards, screen backgrounds

3. Typography & Hierarchical Scale

Poppins is the primary brand font for Tyreplex. It is paired with Inter or SF Pro for technical statistics, specifications, timestamps, and numbers.

Category Font Family Style & Weight Size (Pixels) Examples
Main Metrics Poppins Bold 20px - 24px Outstandings (e.g. ₹ 75,000), total items counts
Page Headers Poppins SemiBold / Bold 18px "Stock Take", "Add Tyre", "Quote Details"
Card Titles Poppins SemiBold 14px - 15px Brand names (e.g. CEAT, MRF), category selections
Tyre Specifications Inter / SF Pro Regular / Medium 11px - 13px Tyre sizes (e.g. 165/70/R14), quantity inputs
Status Badges Inter / SF Pro SemiBold 8px - 10px "Last audited: Yesterday", "Shortage"

4. Media & Upload Specifications

Tyre Product Photos

Tyre product photos must highlight tread patterns and sidewall markers clearly, shot against a solid white background with soft dropped shadows.

Image Upload Containers

The upload visual is styled with a dashed red border, a light pink background, and a camera icon placed inside a circular container.
