# 📘 Tyreplex Brand Design Guidelines
## Brand Identity & Visual Guidelines (Version 1.0)

This document defines the official **Tyreplex Brand Guidelines**, adapting the premium, structured design layout of the 91Wheels guidelines to the visual patterns, color tokens, and workflows extracted from the Tyreplex/Stocktick Figma ecosystem.

---

## 👁️ Brand Vision & Essence

### 1. Our Vision
> **Digitize & Empower Tyre Retail**  
> We simplify and streamline tyre retail operations, stock management, and B2B pricing for tyre dealers and distributors through **Trust**, **Efficiency**, and **Data-driven Clarity**.

### 2. Our Purpose (Why We Exist)
To bridge the gap between traditional tyre retailers and modern digital supply chains. We empower dealers and relationship managers with intelligent mobile-first inventory, instant B2B bidding, and offline-compatible audit tools, turning manual shop management into a streamlined digital experience.

### 3. Core Tagline
> **Smart Audit. Live Bids. Best Deal.**  
> Captures our tripartite value proposition: instant mobile stocktakes, real-time distributor price bidding, and maximizing dealer margins.

---

## 🎭 Brand Archetypes & Tone of Voice

Our brand voice is authoritative, helpful, and highly systematic, structured as a balanced blend of three core archetypes:

| Archetype | Weight | Core Characteristics | Meaning for Creative Expression |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Partner** | **50%** | Collaborative, Helpful, Localized | **Relatable, supportive, down-to-earth** — speaking in a clear, supportive voice that respects the dealer’s daily operations, avoiding complex jargon where simple words work. |
| **The Organizer** | **30%** | Systematic, Precise, Reliable | **Structured, clean, detailed** — organizing complex tyre specifications (DOT, widths, rim sizes) and billing details into high-density, readable layouts. |
| **The Innovator** | **20%** | Tech-forward, Proactive, Efficient | **Pioneering, fresh, active** — showcasing modern features like OCR scanning, instant bidding countdowns, and automated par reordering. |

## 🎨 Color Palette & Swatches

Our colors are selected for high visibility and immediate status recognition in busy, low-light retail shop environments. Color codes must be applied exactly as defined below.

### 1. Primary Colors
*   **Tyreplex Red (`#ED1D24`):** Our high-energy brand accent representing primary actions, urgent CTAs, and shortage indicators.
    *   *RGB:* `237, 29, 36` | *CMYK:* `0, 98, 95, 7`
*   **Tyreplex Dark (`#121726`):** The core background, text, and structure color representing security, authority, and depth.
    *   *RGB:* `18, 23, 38` | *CMYK:* `90, 75, 45, 60`

### 2. Functional Accent Colors
*   **Success Green (`#00B633` / `#00B533`):** Payment success, positive count variances, and verified items.
    *   *RGB:* `0, 182, 51`
*   **Bidding Orange (`#FF6B00`):** Pending bids, active countdown timers, and warning/surplus discrepancies.
    *   *RGB:* `255, 107, 0`
*   **Info Blue (`#0066FF` / `#2563EB`):** Technical specs labels, BNPL credit sheets, and interactive links.
    *   *RGB:* `0, 102, 255`

### 3. Neutral Shades & Backdrops
*   **Slate Gray (`#627085`):** Secondary descriptions, placeholders, and Expected Quantity counts.
*   **Border Gray (`#E5E8EB` / `#D9D9D9`):** 1px card borders, outlines, and page splitters.
*   **Frost White-Blue (`#F7FAFA` / `#F3F4F6`):** Standard screen background to offset content cards.

## ✍️ Typography & Hierarchical Scale

We use a dual-font structure to balance editorial branding with high-density data legibility:

### 1. UI & Brand Font: Poppins
Used for headlines, titles, button labels, and high-emphasis brand marketing components.
*   *Headlines & Metric Totals:* `Poppins Bold`
*   *Card Titles & Primary Buttons:* `Poppins SemiBold`
*   *Body Explanations:* `Poppins Regular`

### 2. Technical & Data Font: Inter / SF Pro
Used for technical tyre specifications (e.g., `185/65/R15`), numeric lists, date-time stamps, and status badges.
*   *Usage:* Ensures monospace-like spacing for numeric columns in data-dense tables.

---

## 📐 Layout & Geometric Radii

Tyreplex mobile layouts operate on a standard **401px - 412px grid width** with **16px margins**.

*   **System Badges & Tags:** `4px` corner radius.
*   **Count Stepper Boxes:** `8px` corner radius.
*   **Standard Cards & Buttons:** `12px` - `16px` corner radius.
*   **Bottom Sheet Panels:** `24px` top corners (mixed layout).
*   **Drag Handle:** `40x4px` with a `2px` corner radius.

---

## 📸 Media & Photography Guidelines

*   **Tyre Product Photography:** Tyres must be placed centrally against a clean white background with a subtle gray shadow, ensuring the tread pattern and sidewall size markings (e.g., section width and aspect ratio) are highly legible.
*   **Casing & Condition:** Segment visual layouts between **New** (clean, crisp tyres), **Used** (showing slight tread wear), and **Retread** (highlighting the remolded tread line).
*   **Image Upload Cards:** Dotted borders (`border-dashed`) with a light pink background (`#FCF2F2`) and camera overlay icons.
