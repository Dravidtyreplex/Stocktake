1. Brand Vision & Purpose

At the heart of the 91Wheels identity is a commitment to transforming how automotive consumers research and buy vehicles in India. We speak to a mobile-first generation that expects swift, clear, and highly personalized digital products.

Simplify Personal Mobility
We make automotive research clear, empowering, and trustworthy by organizing complex technical data into accessible user value.

Core Archetypes (Our Voice)

Archetype Weight Attributes Creative Expression
The Leader 50% Responsible, Organized, Visionary Confident, authoritative, progressive. Speaks decisively without arrogance.
The Sage 30% Wise, Trustworthy, Expert Knowledgeable, reliable, insightful. Shares deep automotive expertise in an accessible voice.
The Creator 10% Innovative, Proactive, Fresh Tech-forward and pioneering. Reimagines traditional research paradigms with fresh formats.

2. Core Colors (Primary & Secondary)

Our color system relies on high-contrast pairings. The primary Nitro Blue drives digital engagement, while Midnight Dark anchors typographic structure.

Primary Colors

Nitro Blue #0165FE Primary logo, CTAs, active highlights
Midnight Dark #142338 Headers, dark backgrounds, high-emphasis text

Secondary & Neutral Colors

Sporty Yellow #F9D816 Active status markers, badges, and accents
Steel Grey #A9AFB5 Divider lines, boundaries, default icon outlines
Titanium Grey #43525E Secondary body copy, subtitles
Frost Silver #DAE1E5 Screen backdrops, light card panels

3. Typography & Hierarchy

Poppins is the primary typeface for 91Wheels. It maintains a clean, geometric line weight that optimizes readability on high-density screens.

Level Font Weight Size (Pixels) Context & Usage
Headlines Bold 24px - 36px Main hero sections, primary titles
Subheads SemiBold 16px - 20px Intermediate page blocks, card labels
Body Text Regular 14px Paragraphs, specifications lists, comparisons
Metadata Regular / Medium 10px - 12px Helper descriptions, time stamps, ratings

4. Visual Signatures & Photography

The Curving Ribbon

Our key visual signature is an organic, curving ribbon line that indicates connectivity and active paths.

[Curving Ribbon Visual Accent - Midnight Backdrop]

Photography Rules

  • Hero Placement: Centralized placement of the vehicle with a 65% White to 35% Asphalt Gray gradient blend.
  • Angles: Mandatory three-quarter shots to maximize vehicle details and structural dimension.
  • Line Iconography: Line weight of 2px with rounded nodes for consistent, minimal icon graphics.
